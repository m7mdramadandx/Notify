# Notify


# Google Play -

[![](https://www.altitude-games.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/get-it-on-google-play.jpg)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ramadan.notify)


Notify App is a simple and awesome noting app. It gives you a quick and simple note in many forms such as (text note, voice note and drawing note) also todo list to make you always in time supported with beautiful dark theme. Taking notes with Notify is easier than any other notepad or memo pad app.
